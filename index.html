📑 Return Preparation Guide

Step-by-step guide to prepare and file GST returns.

1

Prepare Documentation

Gather all invoices, credit notes, debit notes, and payment receipts for the period.

  • Sales invoices (GSTR-1)
  • Purchase invoices (GSTR-2A)
  • Credit/Debit notes
  • Payment records
2

Reconcile Records

Verify that your records match with the portal data.

  • Match invoices with GSTR-2A
  • Check for mismatches
  • Verify payment records
  • Review discrepancies
3

Calculate Tax Liability

Compute the GST liability for the period.

  • Total output tax
  • Less: Input tax credit
  • Net tax payable
  • Add: Interest (if any)
4

File Return

File GSTR-3B before the due date.

  • Login to GST portal
  • Fill GSTR-3B form
  • Verify details
  • Submit & get ARN
5

Make Payment

Pay the due tax and file confirmation.

  • Initiate tax payment
  • Complete payment online
  • Get payment confirmation
  • File final return
6

Archive Records

Maintain records for audit purposes.

  • Save filed returns
  • Keep payment receipts
  • Preserve documents
  • For 6 years minimum
